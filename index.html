SWEDEN



MISSION

To create a sustainable and inclusive society that prioritizes well-being, innovation, and equality for all its citizens. Sweden envisions being a global leader in environmental sustainability, social welfare, and technological advancement, ensuring that future generations enjoy a high quality of life.

Innovative Technology Hub

Sweden is recognized globally as a center for cutting-edge technology and innovation. Home to major tech companies like Spotify and Ericsson, Sweden offers a nurturing ecosystem for startups, focusing on sustainability, research, and development.

Inclusive Welfare System

The Swedish welfare system is built on the principles of universal healthcare, free education, and social security. It ensures equal access to opportunities and resources, contributing to a high standard of living for all citizens.

Environmental Stewardship

Sweden has made significant strides in renewable energy, with over 50% of its energy consumption coming from renewable sources. The country is a global leader in environmental sustainability, setting ambitious goals for carbon neutrality.
